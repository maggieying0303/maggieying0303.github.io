Hi there, thanks for stopping by!

My name is Maggie, and I am a rising senior double-majoring in Information Science (concentration in UX Design) and Economics at Cornell University. This summer, I was an Experience Design intern at Bank of America.

On campus, I am a part of Cornell AppDev, where I collaborate with other talented peers to build digital products for our local community. I also co-teach Intro to Digital Product Design to advocate design education at Cornell.

As a firm believer in simplicity and intentionality, I always strive to follow these two principles in my own design.

When not designing, I can be found editing photos, making collages, and drinking soy latte.
